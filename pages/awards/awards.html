2022
PENTAWARDS: CORE AWARD
The Core Award was created to recognize consistent creative excellence and was awarded for the first time in the history of Pentawards to an agency with 10+ wins in one year. This year was a big milestone for us, as we received a whopping 14 Shortlists. And then, went on to win 5 Golds, 2 Silvers, and 3 Bronzes. Altogether these 10 awards made us the inaugural winner of the Core Award.
2022
ADC AWARDS: THE BOUTIQUE DESIGN STUDIO OF THE YEAR
We were speechless and delighted to win the biggest prize of the night – the Boutique Design Studio Of The Year at ADC Awards. But also, won four more significant awards, such as Gold in Brand / Communication Design for Bnavan’s Brand Identity, Silver in Packaging Design for Nine Wine, Bronze in Packaging Design for Bnavan’s Private Label, and one Merit in Packaging Design for Ani Ice Cream.
